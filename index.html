MSEB Employees Co-op Cr Soc.Ltd. Mumbai consists of the employees of Holding Company, MAHAGENCO, MAHADISCOM & MAHATRANSCO . We are very proud to say that society begins in year 1975 as a seed of a co-operative tree and today after 40 years progress is continued.

A seed sowed by Mr Shripati Bilgi Saheb, Shri V.K.Kulkarni And Mr.K.B.Raut become a big tree having turnover more than Rs. 84 Crores. It also possible only because of our respected past and present Members, Members of Managing Committee and working Staff of this Credit Society.
