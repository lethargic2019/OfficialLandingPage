About us
MSEB Employees Co-op Credit Society Ltd. Mumbai consists of the employees of Holding Company,
MAHAGENCO, MAHADISCOM & MAHATRANSCO .

We are very proud to say that society begins in year 1975 as a seed of a co-operative tree and today after 40 years progress is continued.
A seed sowed by Mr Shripati Bilgi Saheb, Shri V.K.Kulkarni And Mr.K.B.Raut become a big tree having turnover more than Rs. 84 Crores.
It also possible only because of our respected past and present Members, Members of Managing Committee and working Staff of this Credit Society.

Society Managing Committee is always acting with consciousness about rights of members, benefits of members and welfare .We do respect to the suggestion of members, resulted to personal attachment of every member about society. The society is always helpful to fulfilling member dreams, plans and progress.

As everybody knows that today life is very speedy and financially unstable, in this situation information about our credit society and personal financial position with credit society is must. In this consideration, society has made computerised working and recently society started SMS service to convey updates to the members.

Now the next step committee has taken to have this information at any time and anywhere, on the occasion of Republic day of India 26th January 2011, we are launching Society's own Web Site as www.msebhosoc.com This will be helpful to all members regarding information like loans, Fixed deposits Scheme and Society's other Schemes.

We are once again thankful to all the members who are giving valuable co-operation to working committee. You are requested to convey the members to visit on website and share your updates.

Your valuable co-operation in this regard is expected.
